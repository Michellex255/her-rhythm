How it works

Your cycle drives
everything.

Her Rhythm knows what phase you are in today and adapts your entire plan accordingly. Every single day is different — and now your app knows that.

Phase 1
Menstrual
Days 1–5
Lower intensity, anti-inflammatory nutrition. Movement that actually helps with cramps rather than fighting through them.
Phase 2
Follicular
Days 6–13
Your power window. Rising estrogen builds muscle for you. Her Rhythm pushes you to lift heavy and hit personal records right now.
Phase 3
Ovulation
Day ~14
Peak strength and confidence. Her Rhythm flags injury risk from ligament laxity and helps you make the most of this window safely.
Phase 4
Luteal
Days 15–28
Metabolism rises, energy shifts inward. Her Rhythm tells you to eat more and train smarter — because the science says so.
