The problem

Every wellness app was built
for a man's body.

The science has known for decades that women's bodies change dramatically across their cycle. The apps have not caught up. Until now.

01
Training apps give you the same workout every day. But your strength and recovery change by up to 40% across your cycle.
02
Nutrition apps give you the same calories every day. But your metabolism increases 100–300 kcal in your luteal phase alone.
03
Supplement apps give you a one-size stack. But what your body needs on day 6 is completely different to day 24.
How it works

Your cycle drives
everything.

Her Rhythm tracks your phase in real time and adapts your entire wellness plan to match exactly where you are today.

Phase 1
Menstrual
Days 1–5
Lower hormones, lower intensity. Her Rhythm prioritises anti-inflammatory nutrition and gentle movement that actually helps with cramps.
Phase 2
Follicular
Days 6–13
Rising estrogen is anabolic — it builds muscle for you. Her Rhythm pushes you to lift heavy, sprint hard and hit personal records.
Phase 3
Ovulation
Day ~14
Peak strength and energy. Her Rhythm flags injury risk from ligament laxity and helps you maximise this powerful window safely.
Phase 4
Luteal
Days 15–28
Metabolism rises, energy shifts inward. Her Rhythm tells you to eat more, train smarter and rest without guilt — because science says so.
